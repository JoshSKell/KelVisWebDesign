Get Started

Getting started...

Book a meeting.

Upon clicking the Contact tab you will be directed to a form of contact, there you will complete the application and one of our web engineers will schedule a meeting to discuss your needs.

Meeting Day

On meeting day all we ask is to bring yourself and have a list of requirements of what you would want your application to entail and we will take care of the rest.

Development Process

Our development process and duration depends on the outstanding list of requirments and needs for your website, we will keep you updated on the progress of your application throughout this development process. If this is an application that is being built from scratch we will provide you with a demo of the application before it goes live. We have a selection of website templates that you can choose from to get started.

Technical Support and Inquiries

Once the website is created according to your standards, we will hand over all the rights and ownership of the website to you. We will also provide you with a 30 day technical support period where you can contact us for any inquiries or issues that you may have with the website. After the 30 day period you can contact us for any further inquiries or issues that you may have with the website. We value quality and consistency, for a small fee we are happy to continue to be your technical support and this can be seen under our various packages in the pricing section.
